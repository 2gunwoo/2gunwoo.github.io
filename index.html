SBNetwork Instructions

Welcome to SBNetwork! This page provides instructions on how to sign out, delete your account, manage posts, and understand our policies.

How to Sign Out of the App

This guide explains how to sign out of the app and manage your account data.

  1. Open the app on your Android device.
  2. Go to the "Settings" or "Profile" section (usually found in the navigation menu).
  3. Look for the "Sign Out" button and tap it.
  4. You will be signed out of the app, and you’ll return to the login screen.

How to Delete a Post

You can delete your own messages (posts) in the app’s chat channels. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and navigate to the chat channel where your message is located.
  2. Find the message you want to delete (e.g., "chat-test" posted by you).
  3. Long-press the message or tap the three-dot menu next to it.
  4. Select "Delete" from the options.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted. The message will be removed from the channel.
  6. Note: Only messages you posted can be deleted. Contact support if you need help with others’ posts.

How to Edit a Post

You can edit your own messages (posts) in the app’s chat channels. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and go to the chat channel containing your message.
  2. Locate your message (e.g., "chat-test").
  3. Long-press the message or tap the three-dot menu next to it.
  4. Choose "Edit" from the options.
  5. Modify the text in the provided text box and save your changes.
  6. The updated message will appear in the channel with an "edited" label.
  7. Note: Editing is only available for your own messages and may have a time limit after posting.
