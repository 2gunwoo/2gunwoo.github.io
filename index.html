This guide explains how to sign out of the app and manage your account data.

Steps to Sign Out

  1. Open the app on your Android device.
  2. Go to the "Settings" or "Profile" section (usually found in the navigation menu).
  3. Look for the "Sign Out" button and tap it.

Account Deletion

This app uses Google Sign-In for authentication. We do not store additional user data beyond what is provided by Google. To delete your account data:

  1. Visit Google Account Management.
  2. Sign in with the Google account you used for this app.
  3. Follow the instructions to remove access or delete your Google account entirely.
  4. Note: Deleting your Google account will remove access to all services linked to it, including this app.
